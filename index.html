Aparna Constructions

Aparna Dharti provides customers a tranquil, peaceful and spacious ecosystem where they can build their dream homes. The property has amenities like bore water supply connection, harvesting chambers for rain water percolation, electricity powered by armored cables, 60’/ 40’ feet roads within the project, solar powered boundary wall fencing and avenue plantation solar fencing drainage connections; amongst other attributed.

Aditya Constructions

Aditya Construction Company India (P) Ltd., is a reputed enterprise focused on developing luxurious gated communities with the best of amenities. Established in the year 2002, the company has developed numerous luxury apartments, villas at prime locations in Hyderabad. Over the years, Aditya has earned a reputation for quality and innovation. All its projects come with ample greenery, spacious and modern design and the best of amenities and of course, luxury. The company takes particular care in ensuring that its projects feature perfect vaasthu and a clear title so as to give buyers a hassle-free and happy ownership. Today, owning an Aditya Property is associated with prestige as virtually every project the company has developed till date has become a landmark in its respective surroundings.

Candor Constructions

Candor Construction Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 30 August 2016. It is classified as Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Ernakulam. Its authorized share capital is Rs. 100,000 and its paid up capital is Rs. 100,000. It is inolved in Business activities n.e.c. Candor Construction Private Limited's Annual General Meeting (AGM) was last held on 31 December 2020 and as per records from Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), its balance sheet was last filed on 31 March 2020. Directors of Candor Construction Private Limited are Abdul Rahim Kudilil Nazruddin, Kudilil Abdulrahiman Naziruddin and Sameena Abdul Rahim.

Casey Towers

We are proud to count on the Industries leading construction companies as our customers. Continuous investment in our hire fleet which consists of the German manufactured Liebherr & Wolffkran models has allowed us to maintain our position as a leading supplier

My Home Jewels

My Home Jewel is indeed a jewel in Hyderabad’s real estate landscape. It is Hyderabad’s first housing project to be awarded with the coveted ‘CRISIL SIX STAR’ rating. What does CRISIL rating mean? It is a top-rate endorsement of My Home’s high standards with regard to timely delivery, financial stability, quality of legal documentation and track record.
